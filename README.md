1. Dataset Used

2. Selected Variables   
 • Behavioral (SadOrHopeless): Recoded as Success (1) and Failure (2).   
 • Continuous (Height): Measured in meters.  
3. Benchmark Values   
 • Proportion Benchmark (p_0): 0.30   
 • Mean Benchmark (\mu_0): 1.70 meters.  
4. Short Project Question   
 • Proportion Analysis: Is the proportion of students who feel sad or hopeless significantly different from the benchmark of 0.30?   
 • Mean Analysis: Is the mean height of students significantly different from the benchmark of 1.70 meters?  
5. Short Final Conclusion   
 • Emotional Health: The proportion of sadness is consistent with the 30\% benchmark (P > 0.05); the null hypothesis failed to be rejected.   
 • Physical Measurement: There is a statistically significant decrease in height; the average height (1.69m) is significantly lower than the 1.70m benchmark (P < 0.05)
